onPHP

onPHP - an extraordinarily rapid framework for the object-oriented programing on PHP 5. onPHP enables unique ability to provide and repeatedly surpass all speed of the sites developed on by the «pure» PHP, combining it with unsurpassed labour productivity of developer, here on orders decreasing the amount of errors and volume of debugging.

will benefit from onPHP

  • sites with the enormous loading and permanent update of information;
  • sites with the promoted requirements to reliability at the limited budget;
  • unique sites «not written» into the prokrustovo's bed of standard CMS-engines;
  • control by content systems which are easily created on the base of onPHP.

GPL

onPHP spreads in source codes subject to the conditions of popular license — GPL, and it means that you will never run into dependence on a developer, and will be always able to make the alterations.
And if you have the own projects - disemboguing in development of onPHP, you will save them for descendants, not giving to remain within the framework of proprietary project.

rapid development

All of the tools, entering in onPHP, is created «from life» at development of some sites; many good patterns which most developers it is had to use on PHP are therefore realized, «reinventing a wheel».
Library authors (also onPHP's developers) are also very like to release the time.

performance of the developed web sites

Burdens costs on the use of onPHP are taken to the minimum, and the built-in effective mechanism of the scalable caching subsystem allows to be ahead of decisions on by the «pure» PHP on speed works of site, or it is considerably to reduce the vehicles requirements for the set speed.

intuitional rapid teaching

It is possible to notice that on our site not too much document. onPHP is so organicly continues the best in the initial project of PHP, that allows to avoid «get bogged» in a project, and straight «lies down in a hand».

faultless purposeful development

Most errors appears at «transfusion from empty in empty» - input validation, transformation of data models for DB's relations, identification of users, differentiating of rights, etc.
Components and layers of abstraction of onPHP decide these tasks in a general view, allowing to be concentrated on creation.
XML prototyping allows to automatically generate the most «non-creative code» - (determinations of classes, DB schemas, DAO-classes, etc.). As a result reduction of volume of own code does a project «visible».

lightness of support

Web sites are created, as a rule, without a project document. When to you somebody's site developed by year-two back hand gets in a revision, it is often simpler to rewrite it anew, by what to try to understand.
With onPHP this far simpler - eventually, an existent site can be used as a demonstration prototype, quickly and unnoticed shifting to onPHP. And the participants of your command will take your code as a hint, seeing the best lines with PHP fresh eyes.

and finally, a fly in the ointment: onPHP disciplines a developer

onPHP - object-oriented, and partly it's patterns are «spyed» from Java, Smalltalk, Zonnon, and not only.
Enabling creation considerably more difficult guided projects, by what usually accepted in PHP, onPHP by the spirit and structure induces a developer to «think, before to do». And a developer is here forced gradually to promote the trade.
